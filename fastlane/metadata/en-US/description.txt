AquaPulse is a clean, zero-noise hydration tracker that helps you stay consistently hydrated. Log water intake, set daily goals, and get gentle reminders throughout the day.

Features:
• Simple one-tap water logging
• Customizable daily hydration goals
• Smart reminders that adapt to your schedule
• Intake history and weekly trends
• Multiple drink types (water, coffee, tea, juice)
• Widget support for quick logging
