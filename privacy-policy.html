AquaPulse Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Data Collection

AquaPulse by Abraham Oladotun Foundation NFP stores all your hydration data locally on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers.

Third-Party Services

  • Google AdMob — Displays advertisements in the free version. Google Privacy Policy
  • RevenueCat — Manages premium subscriptions. RevenueCat Privacy Policy

Advertising

The free version displays banner advertisements powered by Google AdMob. On iOS, we request App Tracking Transparency (ATT) permission. If declined, non-personalized ads are shown. Premium subscribers see no ads.

In-App Purchases

Premium subscriptions are processed by Apple App Store or Google Play Store. We do not access your payment information.

Children's Privacy

AquaPulse is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Data Deletion

Delete all data at any time from Settings → Delete all data.

Changes

We may update this policy. Changes take effect immediately upon posting within the app.

Contact

Questions? Email privacy@abrahamoladotun.org
